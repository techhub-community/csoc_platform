What is Codeshack Summer of Code ?

CodeShack Summer of Code is an impactful program organized by CodeShack, the technical community of Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology, Bengaluru. It consists of two phases: a mentorship program and a hackathon. During the mentorship phase, participants are paired with experienced mentors who guide them across various tech domains like Web Development, App Development, DSA and UI/UX Designing . In the hackathon, teams collaborate to create innovative projects. This program fuels personal growth, fosters innovation, and empowers aspiring developers.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • About Mentorship?

    This mentorship is a 2 months event where participants will collaborate on creating a functioning software by the end of the event.

  • Who can enroll for the program?

    This event is open for both first and second year students.

  • Mode of Event?

    Phase I will be conducted online
    Phase II will be conducted offline

  • How and What to Submit?

    You all have to submit GitHub Repository link as well as deployed link of the website or APK in case of App. Deployment/APK will be preferred for evaluation.

  • How to register?

    All you need is to register yourself on the portal with team members

  • Usage of API and packages?

    Yes, usage of APIs and third party packages is allowed but it is advised to build things from scratch

  • Will there be swags?

    Yes, Participants and winners will receive swags, certificates, and much more!

  • Have more questions?

    Reach us directly at Codeshack WhatsApp Group, we would be happy to help you. You all can also contact your respective mentors.
